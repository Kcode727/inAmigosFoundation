Our Journey

How InAmigos started
and operates

InAmigos Foundation was established on September 23, 2020, by Founder & CEO Mr. Govind Shukla. Licensed under Section 8 of the Central Government’s Companies Act, 2013, we operate as a dedicated company for social welfare, ensuring high structural transparency.

Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, our operations extend nationwide. We have built an active network of over 200 volunteers across 28 states, engaging youth in ground-level campaigns and digital advocacy for critical causes.

We are audited, regulated, and verified, providing full corporate trust and reassurance to donors and corporate CSR partners.

Our Mission

To deliver self-sustaining positive change across underprivileged sectors through structured programs in food distribution, child education, women skill workshops, and animal welfare drives.

Our Vision

To foster an inclusive, empathetic, and empowered community where basic needs are protected, ecosystems are conserved, and every youth is equipped to shape a brighter future.
